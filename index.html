Refath Bari -- Writing Samples

You can find some of my writing samples here. Some were written for Brooklyn Tech's The Survey, or as a part of a class.

Ten Years A Student

A personal narrative written for 10th grade English.

Cultural Exposure

Written for a project on Cultural Diffusion between Eastern and Western cultures.

An Unexpected Victory

Written for Brooklyn Tech's The Survey. Depicts my team's victory at Tech's hackathon and my subsequent winning of the "Subject Field Expertise" at Brooklyn's Regional Hackathon.

July 15th, 2017 was a bad morning

A personal anectode written in the first person. Recounts my personal epiphany as a young immigrant, as I come to terms with my prejudices.

SHSAT #1: A Dangerous Cycle

A heavily researched article I wrote and revised 4 times for the Survey. I compiled data from the New York Times and NYC's Open Data initiative to prove multiple findings regarding NYC's specialized high schools.

The SHSAT II: A Fatal Plan

The second addition to my three-part series on the SHSAT and Specialized High Schools.

About

Refath Bari is a rising Junior at Brooklyn Tech. He is the Editor at The Survey, Brooklyn Tech's newspaper, where he is currently heading the redesign of The Survey's website using jQuery, React, and Vue. He earned a perfect math score on the SAT, and led his team to a win at Tech's Hackathon and an award for "Subject Field Expertise" at Brooklyn's Regional Hackathon for the app "Rescuer".
